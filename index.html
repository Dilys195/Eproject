About Mrs. Patrona

Mrs. Patrona is a veteran with a passionate love for animals, especially dogs, cats, and rabbits. With many years of experience in animal care and behavior research, she has accumulated valuable knowledge about various dog breeds. Not only is she an animal enthusiast, but she is also a talented photographer with a rich collection of images showcasing different animals.

Mrs. Patrona decided to create this website to share her passion and knowledge with the community. The website serves not only as a treasure trove of information about dog breeds but also as a valuable resource for those who love animals and want to learn more about the world of dogs. With her dedication and experience, she hopes to provide everyone with useful information and vibrant images of dog breeds.
